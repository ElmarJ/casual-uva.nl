We are a collective of employees on temporary contracts, including representatives from a range of departments, at the University of Amsterdam. Please sign our petition for permanent contract for junior lecturers: <!--StartFragment-->

[Structural work deserves Permanent Contracts!](https://cryptpad.fr/form/#/2/form/view/w0VFSSQb3LshCmjyGGK-f0+kwFxabLl91z+N4VU8gec/)

<!--EndFragment-->



<!--StartFragment-->

On **April 14 from 16:00-17:00**, on the **Roeterseiland lawn behind the L building**, we will hand over the petition to Prof. Peter-Paul Verbeek, who has agreed to represent the UvA’s Executive Board (CvB).<!--StartFragment-->

Our goal is to submit a petition with the most signatures possible. For that, we need your help.

 

Collective action depends on sustained engagement and structured communication. Your continued involvement at this stage is crucial. There are three things you can do in particular:

1. **Please spread the petition as much as you can!** This means talking to colleagues, presenting the petition to students, and asking your department’s Point of Contact (PC) if you can help with flyering efforts. All necessary materials are attached to this email.
2. **Please attend the handover event, and invite colleagues and students to do so!** Your attendance is crucial, as the more people who show support, the more impact we’ll have on the university’s decisions. We wish there to be at least 300 comrades in attendance to show that we are well-organized and unified!
